Olympic Barbell

A men's Olympic bar is a metal bar that is 2.2 metres (7.2 ft) long and weighs 20 kilograms (44 lb). The outer ends are 50 millimetres (1.96 in) in diameter, while the grip section is 28 millimetres (1.1 in) in diameter, and 1.31 metres (4.3 ft) in length. Our barbells are produced with the outmost attention to quality and thus durability.

We estimate our product's max weight load at 750kg, making it one of the heighest amongst top 10 of the world's best manufacturers. Our bars not only found their way to top franchise gyms but also powerlifting, crossfit and strong-men events etching an indelible mark on all who used them, competed with them and held them in their hands.

Curly Bar

Curly bar just might be the most universal of the entire variety of bars used in modern resistance training. Although primarly used in different bicep exercises it can also be used for tricep development, shoulder presses and even chest bench press. The product that we offer has a top weight limit of 160 kg, which is very high for any curly barbell.

Our bar is manufactured from top quality registered trademark reinforced steel alloy. Compared to its weight limit it only weighs about 7 kg, its total length is 158 cm. Both the ends and the main section have a diameter of 30 mm (which is exactly the diameter of our free weight dumbbells shaft). The main section measures 95 cm in length which leaves 31.5 cm at each end.
